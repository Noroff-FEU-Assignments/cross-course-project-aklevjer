Discover the story of rainy days

Who we are

Rainy Days was founded in 2010 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who were tired of subpar rain gear. They set out to create a line of rain jackets that not only kept you dry but also kept you comfortable, even in the most extreme conditions.

Over the years, Rainy Days has continued to innovate and push the boundaries of what's possible in rain gear. We belive in creating comfortable and dry experiences without compromise, and constantly explore new materials and technologies to achieve this.

Why us

At Rainy Days, we're dedicated to providing high-quality rainwear that's both functional and fashionable. Our mission is to equip our customers with the best rain jackets, so they can confidently embrace any weather and explore the world around them.

We strive to make a positive impact on the environment by using sustainable materials and manufacturing processes. At Rainy Days, we are committed to pushing the comfort zone and empowering our customers to live life the fullest, rain or shine.
